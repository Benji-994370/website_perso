Workflows & Automations

GTM Research Flow

A single form submission triggers a fully automated research pipeline. The system creates a tracking record, duplicates a branded slide template, scrapes the target company's website, runs AI-powered analysis, and generates a complete go-to-market research deck — delivered by email within minutes, with zero manual work.

Tools: n8n, Airtable, Google Slides, Firecrawl, Google Gemini, Gmail

[Photo / video coming soon]

LinkedIn Influencer Engagement System

The system tracks targeted influencers on LinkedIn and automatically scrapes their latest posts every day. Each post is organized in a table with author, date, and engagement data. An AI agent generates a personalized comment for each post that can be reviewed and edited. Once approved, a single click posts the comment directly under the LinkedIn post.

Tools: n8n, Airtable, Apify, OpenAI, Slack

[Photo / video coming soon]

CRM — Automated Meeting Prep

When a contact books a meeting, the system automatically enriches their profile from LinkedIn and their company website, then generates a complete meeting brief — including company research, a suggested call structure, and personalized talking points. Everything is ready in the CRM before the call starts.

Tools: n8n, Airtable, Kuration, Google Gemini, Google Calendar

[Photo / video coming soon]

CRM — Follow-up Presentation & Email

After a meeting, the system processes the call transcript, generates a summary, and creates a personalized follow-up presentation based on what was discussed. It then drafts a tailored email with the presentation attached and sends it automatically — turning every meeting into a professional follow-up in minutes.

Tools: n8n, Airtable, Google Gemini, Google Slides, Google Drive, Gmail

[Photo / video coming soon]

LinkedIn Comment Scraper to Email Outreach

Paste a LinkedIn post URL into Slack, and the system scrapes every commenter, deduplicates against existing contacts, enriches each profile, and uses AI to generate personalized email sequences — sent automatically through a dedicated outreach platform. This single workflow generated 90 opportunities from 3,000 contacts at a 4% reply rate.

Tools: n8n, Slack, Apify, Airtable, Kuration, OpenAI, Instantly

Results

These automation systems transformed Kuration's go-to-market operations. The LinkedIn-to-email pipeline alone generated 90 qualified opportunities from 3,000 contacts at a 4% reply rate. The GTM research flow reduced company analysis time from 2 hours to under 5 minutes per prospect. The CRM system eliminated manual meeting prep and follow-up entirely, saving over 20 hours per week across the sales team.
