AI implementation

When your workflow benefits from AI, I integrate LLM steps directly into your automations. This includes:

  • Custom prompt engineering: Carefully crafted prompts tailored to your specific use case and data format
  • Output parsing: Structured extraction from AI responses for reliable downstream use
  • Multi-step AI chains: Breaking complex tasks into sequential AI operations for better accuracy
  • Classification & routing: Using AI to categorize inputs and route them to the right workflow branch
  • Content generation: AI-powered drafts for emails, reports, or summaries based on your data

AI is added only when it genuinely improves the workflow, not for the sake of it. I'll always discuss the trade-offs (cost, latency, reliability) before implementing.

Custom platform development

When off-the-shelf tools aren't enough, I build custom platforms tailored to your exact needs:

  • Internal dashboards: Real-time operational dashboards pulling data from multiple sources
  • Custom web apps: Lightweight tools for specific workflows (intake forms, approval systems, data processors)
  • Automation interfaces: Custom UIs that sit on top of your automation workflows for easy team access
  • API wrappers: Simplifying complex APIs into clean interfaces your team can use
  • Data management tools: Custom interfaces to organize, edit, and clean up your data

All platforms are built with modern web technologies, deployed to reliable hosting, and come with documentation so your team can manage them.

How it works

  1. Discovery & scoping: We map your full process, identify all tools involved, and define the automation requirements. I'll provide a detailed scope document and quote.
  2. Architecture design: I design the full system architecture (tools, connections, logic, AI steps) and share it for your approval.
  3. Access & setup: You provide access to all required tools, APIs, and platforms.
  4. Build in phases: I build the automation in logical phases, sharing progress at each milestone so you can validate along the way.
  5. Testing: Thorough testing with real data, including edge cases and error scenarios.
  6. Deploy & monitor: Live deployment with monitoring setup and alerting for any failures.
  7. Documentation & handoff: Complete system documentation, architecture diagram, and video walkthrough.

Frequently asked questions

How do you determine the price?
Price depends on the number of tools involved, complexity of logic, whether AI is needed, and the expected volume. After the discovery call, I provide a detailed quote with a clear breakdown.
What AI models do you use?
I primarily use OpenAI (ChatGPT) and Anthropic (Claude) through their APIs, integrated directly into Make, n8n, or custom scripts. I'll recommend the best model for your specific use case based on cost, speed, and accuracy.
Who pays for AI API usage?
You do. AI API costs (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) are part of your operational costs, like your automation platform subscription. I'll estimate monthly costs during scoping so there are no surprises.
Can you integrate with our internal APIs?
Yes. If your tools expose APIs, I can connect to them. I'll need documentation or access to your API specs during the scoping phase.
What if the project scope changes?
Scope changes are quoted separately. I'll always flag when a request goes beyond the agreed scope and provide a quote before proceeding.
What happens after 30 days?
The 30-day free maintenance covers adjustments and bug fixes. After that, you can opt for ongoing maintenance at 10% of project value per month, or manage it yourself using the documentation provided. Tool and API subscription costs are always your responsibility.
