Simple Automation

No-code workflows that save you hours every week

3,500 HKD

per automation

Who this is for

You have a process that follows the same steps every time, and you're tired of doing it manually. You need to connect 2-3 tools with a straightforward, linear workflow. No code required.

  • Small businesses automating routine tasks for the first time
  • Teams with clear, defined processes that don't need complex logic
  • Anyone spending hours on repetitive copy-paste between tools
  • Companies looking for quick wins before committing to larger projects

What's included

  • Discovery session (30-60 min) to understand your process and goals
  • Custom workflow design and mapping shared for your approval
  • Build using no-code tools (Make or n8n)
  • Connect up to 3 tools in a single workflow
  • Linear workflow logic (A → B → C)
  • Testing with real data and deployment to your systems
  • Complete documentation (how it works, what triggers it, how to monitor)
  • Quick Loom video walkthrough of the automation
  • 30 days free maintenance for small adjustments (within 4 business days)

What's not included

  • Database design or architecture
  • More than 3 tool connections per workflow
  • Complex branching logic or conditional workflows
  • Team training sessions (available as add-on)

Need any of these? Check out Complex Automation instead.

How it works

  1. Book a discovery call: We discuss your process, pain points, and desired outcome. I'll confirm if Simple Automation is the right fit.
  2. Provide tool access: You grant me access to the tools we'll connect (Make/n8n + your existing platforms).
  3. Workflow design: I map out the automation and share the design for your approval before building.
  4. Build & test: I build the automation and test it with your real data to make sure everything works.
  5. Deploy: We deploy the automation live and run tests together to verify everything.
  6. Documentation: You receive complete docs and a video walkthrough so you can monitor and understand your automation.

Delivery timeline

3-5 business days after scope is defined and tool access is provided.

Timeline starts once we've agreed on the workflow design and you've granted access to all necessary tools. Delays in providing access or scope changes will extend the timeline accordingly.

Example use cases

  • CRM auto-update: Form submitted → Create/update contact in HubSpot → Send Slack notification to the team
  • Lead routing: New lead enters via Typeform → Check criteria → Assign to correct sales rep in CRM
  • Invoice notifications: Payment received in Stripe → Update tracking in Airtable → Send confirmation email
  • Content publishing: Blog post published → Share to LinkedIn → Log in tracking spreadsheet

Frequently asked questions

What tools do you work with?
I primarily use Make and n8n for no-code automations. I can connect most popular tools: HubSpot, Airtable, Notion, Slack, Google Workspace, Stripe, Typeform, and many more.
Do I need to buy tool subscriptions?
Yes. You're responsible for subscription costs for the automation platform (Make/n8n) and any connected tools. I'll recommend the appropriate plan based on your workflow volume.
What happens after 30 days?
The 30-day free maintenance covers small adjustments and bug fixes. After that, you can opt for ongoing maintenance at 10% of project value per month, or manage it yourself using the documentation provided. Tool subscription costs (Make, Zapier, n8n) are always your responsibility.
Can I request changes during the build?
Minor adjustments during the build are fine. Significant scope changes (adding tools, changing logic) will be quoted as a separate automation.
What if my workflow needs more than 3 tools?
That falls under Complex Automation (custom quote). I'll let you know during discovery if your needs exceed the Simple Automation scope.
