Who this is for

You want hands-on collaboration. Instead of sending requirements back and forth, I come to your office and we build together. By 5pm, you'll have multiple working automations deployed and your team trained to use them.

What's included

  • Full-day on-site consulting (10am - 5pm, 1hr lunch break)

  • Priority ranking of automations by impact and effort
  • Real-time workflow building: you watch and learn as I build
  • Deploy multiple automations on the same day

How the day works

  1. Morning, Process audit (10am-12pm): I sit with your team, observe your workflows, and identify every automation opportunity. We prioritize by impact.
  2. Afternoon, Build & deploy (1pm-4pm): I build the automations live, explaining as I go. You and your team can ask questions and learn in real time.
  3. Wrap-up, Training & docs (4pm-5pm): We test everything together. I train your team on monitoring and basic maintenance. You get documentation for everything built.

Delivery timeline

Same day. Automations are built and deployed during the session.

Minimum 1 day engagement, with the option to book additional consecutive days for larger scopes: more flows to build, team training, or complex processes that need extra time. The more days booked, the more we can tackle.

What can you build in one day?

  • 3-5 simple automations connecting your existing tools (CRM updates, Slack notifications, email sequences)
  • 1-2 medium automations with some logic (lead routing, data sync between platforms)
  • Process audit + 2-3 automations if starting from scratch and needing to identify opportunities first

The exact output depends on your tools, access, and complexity. We'll set expectations in the morning audit.

Frequently asked questions

Where are you based?
I'm based in Hong Kong and available on-site anywhere in HK. For locations outside Hong Kong, contact me to discuss availability and travel arrangements.
Can I book multiple days?
Yes. Multi-day engagements are ideal for larger teams or more complex automation needs. Each additional day builds on the previous one.
Do I need to prepare anything?
Have your tool logins ready (CRM, email, project management, etc.) and gather your team members who are involved in the processes you want to automate. That's it. The audit covers the rest.
What if we need more complex work after the day?
Any automation that can't be completed in the day session can be continued as a Simple or Complex Automation project. I'll provide a quote during the wrap-up.
What if we need more than one day?
No problem, just book additional consecutive days. If we identify more flows to build, need extra training time, or want to tackle more complex processes, we extend. We'll discuss scope during the morning audit so you know exactly what to expect.
Why is this rate limited?
This is an introductory rate while I build my consulting practice in Hong Kong. The rate will increase as demand grows. Book now to lock in this pricing.
What happens after 30 days?
The 30-day free maintenance covers adjustments and bug fixes. After that, you can opt for ongoing maintenance at 10% of project value per month, or manage it yourself using the documentation provided. Tool subscription costs are always your responsibility.
