About Me

Most businesses don't need more tools. They need processes that actually work together.

I'm Ben Audry. I help businesses figure out which of their processes should be running on autopilot, and then I make it happen. No jargon, no over-engineering. Just systems that save you real time.

Over the past 5+ years, I've worked with 15+ companies across Asia, Europe, and North America, from startups to established businesses. I've seen what slows teams down, and I know how to fix it. Lead generation, sales ops, client onboarding, reporting, if it's repetitive, I've probably automated it before.

My approach: I start by understanding how your team actually works today, find where time is being wasted, and design systems that remove those bottlenecks. Every project starts with a conversation, not a sales pitch.
